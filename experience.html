MY EXPERIENCE

Overall, I get all incredible experience since I come to UiTM Segamat.

  • Komander Kesatria: I joined Komander Kesatria because I wanted to feel different than any other student. I gained so many experiences and also met some really unique friends.

  • Running Crew: I joined the Running Crew to challenge myself physically and mentally. It taught me the importance of teamwork, perseverance, and time management. Along the way, I also formed great bonds with my team members and learned to enjoy the journey as much as the finish line.

  • Sports Club: Being part of the Sports Club allowed me to stay active and explore different types of sports. I gained leadership skills by organizing events, developed discipline through regular practice, and discovered the value of sportsmanship in both victories and losses.
